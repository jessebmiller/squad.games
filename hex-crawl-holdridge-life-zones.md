# Hex Crawling with Holdridge Life Zones

(Holdridge Life
Zones)[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Holdridge_life_zones] are cool.

Some set of rules to determine how and when the terrain changes, in
reasonably realistinc ways, with no prep. A hex crawl oracle.

Temp goes up at lower elevations and closer to equator.

percipitation/humidity is random? Or more rain closer to coast?

## How do we get coastlines?

* High rainfall is more likely to be next to the coast
* High elevation is less likely to be next to the coast (large lake if
  higher than sea level)
* Elevation is coldness - distance from equator. This takes into
  account the fact that a low temp is either due to distance from
  equator, or elevation
