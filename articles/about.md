Squad Games is a tabletop role playing game community and production
studio created and organized by Jesse B. Miller. We create in the
open, sharing ideas, prototypes, and play testing uncomfortably
early. We invite collaboration and ideas from everyone who's got
them. Join the [Discord](https://discord.gg/Sc6vjTVbe5) and jump in.

More concretely:
* [Squad Games Community Discord](https://discord.gg/Sc6vjTVbe5) where
  you can get to know folks, keep up with what we're doing, and jump
  in and participate!
* [Articles](/) currated by Jesse B. Miller. If you'd like to write
  for Squad Games please reach out in the Community Discord.
* [Games](/games) in various stages of development from nascent ideas
  to play test documents to completed games.
