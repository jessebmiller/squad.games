Play is a way to get experience with someting while limiting it's
scope of consequesce. We play fight buy limiting how badly we hurt
each other. We play Diplomacy and (hope) we know that we are only
lying to each other until the game ends.

---

Game rules are there to set up the scope and nature of consequence
reduction. In a RPG we roll dice rather than fight.

For example sitting around a table rather than having to be up on our
feet in order to "play house"
