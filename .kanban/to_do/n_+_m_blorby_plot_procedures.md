## THE UNIFYING THEORY OF 2+2

The absolute best way to get the audience to care about the story is
to get them involved with the story; to get them actively thinking
about the story. And the best way to do that is to make them work for
the story.

In other words, don’t show your audience FOUR. Show them 2 + 2 and
make them do the math.

https://thealexandrian.net/wordpress/49688/roleplaying-games/random-gm-tip-22-storytelling

Create a game procedure that gives the table or GM a random N and a
random M and let them do the math of N + M to get them involved with
the story. If it's innevitable then everyone should come to the same
conclusion, or close enough to be interesting.
