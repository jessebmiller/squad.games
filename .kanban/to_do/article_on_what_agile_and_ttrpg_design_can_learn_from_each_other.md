# Article on what agile and ttrpg design can learn from each other
---

Card writing, story writing, kanban writing similarly to ttrpg rule
writing is about causing specific conversations and shared
understandings.

A shared understanding of business goals, context, user needs context,
and driving towards an implementable solution etc is the goal of
writing cards, design docs, product requirements for agile teams and
the procedures for the conversation are things like planning poker,
tee shirt sizing, etc.

A shared understanding of the fictional position, character contexts,
goals, motivations, plot, story, narrative, and driving towards the
story we want to experience (tell, watch, etc.) and the rules of the
game are the procedures for that conversation.
