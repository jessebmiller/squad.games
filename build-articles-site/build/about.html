What is Squad Games?

Squad Games is a tabletop role playing game community and production studio created and organized by Jesse B. Miller. We create in the open, sharing ideas, prototypes, and play testing uncomfortably early. We invite collaboration and ideas from everyone who's got them. Join the Discord and jump in.

More concretely:

  • Squad Games Community Discord where you can get to know folks, keep up with what we're doing, and jump in and participate!
  • Articles currated by Jesse B. Miller. If you'd like to write for Squad Games please reach out in the Community Discord.
  • Games in various stages of development from nascent ideas to play test documents to completed games.
