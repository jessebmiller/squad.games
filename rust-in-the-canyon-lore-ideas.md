## The apocalypse

### AI

* Modern tech, primative infrastructure
* Not enough people to perate the vast supply chains required for
  modern civilization
* Deliberate removal of global power and communications systems to
  remove the environment that allowed AIs to take over
* Vegas, Iceland, Norway, Seattle, taken over by embodied AIs who
  maintain renewable power sources and maintain hardware for
  themselves to run on. How long can this last, are they able to set
  up the supply chains needed for manufacturing new hardware for
  themselves indeffinitely? Their current hardware will fail from old
  age at some point.
