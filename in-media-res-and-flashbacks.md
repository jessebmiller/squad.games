# Flashbacks are underutalized

Let's make games that thrust the players into a situation **before
they even create characters**. Fully in media res, for the mechanical
part of the game as well as the fictional part. We know nothing of
these people, nothing of the situation they find themselves in,
nothing of how they got there, just that they are angry, frightentd,
lustful, extatic, whatever, and that they are running, hiding,
driving, searching, whatever.

The game rules and procedures are then all we need, along with the
conversation to discover who the characters are, what they are doing
there, and how it all came to be.

Let's introduce our blades in the dark crew with them all, badass and
grizzled, in court accused of something. Let's then discover how they
got there with flashbacks to scores, downtime etc.
